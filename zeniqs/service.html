Our Services

Our Services

End-to-End Semiconductor & Embedded Engineering

ASIC / Semiconductor Design

We design application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) and custom semiconductor IP tailored to your product requirements. Whether you need a low-power sensor hub, a high-speed data processing engine, or a domain-specific accelerator, our team handles the full design flow.

What We Offer

  • RTL Design & Verification
  • FPGA Prototyping & Validation
  • Synthesis, Place & Route
  • Timing Closure & Sign-off
  • Tape-out Support
  • Post-silicon Validation

Embedded Systems Development

Our embedded engineers develop robust firmware and software for microcontrollers, microprocessors, and SoCs across a wide range of architectures. We work across the full embedded stack from bootloaders and drivers to application logic and communication protocols.

What We Offer

  • Firmware Architecture & Development (C/C++, Assembly)
  • BSP & Device Driver Development
  • RTOS Integration (FreeRTOS, Zephyr, VxWorks, etc.)
  • Communication Protocols (UART, SPI, I2C, CAN, Ethernet, USB, BLE, Wi-Fi)
  • OTA Update Frameworks
  • Power Optimization & Battery Management

Hardware / PCB Design

From schematic capture to production-ready PCB layout, we design hardware that performs reliably in demanding environments. Our designs are optimized for signal integrity, EMC compliance, and manufacturability.

What We Offer

  • Schematic Capture & PCB Layout (Multi-layer, High-Speed)
  • Component Selection & BOM Optimization
  • Signal & Power Integrity Analysis
  • DFM/DFT Reviews
  • Prototype Build & Bring-Up Support

AI at the Edge / Embedded AI

We help product teams deploy machine learning and AI models directly onto resource-constrained edge hardware. From model optimization to hardware-accelerated inference, we bridge the gap between AI research and real-world embedded deployment.

What We Offer

  • TensorFlow Lite
  • ONNX Model Optimization & Quantization
  • NPU / DSP Integration
  • Edge AI Hardware Selection & Platform Design
  • Real-time Inference Pipeline Development

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Goals

Our Mission

We aspire to become the beacon of innovation, delivering the most valuable, leading-edge technologies while fostering a community of likeminded professionals, who play an instrumental role in the 'Make in India' initiative.

Our Vision

Our Vision is to be one of the leading technology fabless chip development & embedded engineering organization, in Machine type communication, Mechatronics and Automotive industries. Committed to promote Make in India initiative.

Our Goal

To achieve fully functional SoC/ASIC Integrated chip of family called MATOMAC for Machine – to – Machine communication Technologies such as NB-IoT, LTE-M, CAT1, and CAT14 with 5G and 4G fall back by 2028.

ASIC / Semiconductor Design

We design application-specific integrated circuits (ASICs) and custom semiconductor IP tailored to your product requirements. Whether you need a low-power sensor hub, a high-speed data processing engine, or a domain-specific accelerator, our team handles the full design flow.

Embedded Systems Development

Our embedded engineers develop robust firmware and software for microcontrollers, microprocessors, and SoCs across a wide range of architectures. We work across the full embedded stack from bootloaders and drivers to application logic and communication protocols.

Hardware / PCB Design
